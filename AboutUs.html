About Us

What is this website about?

Nowadays, as choices and types of electronic gadget are getting wider, and more advance, it’s getting harder for people to choose which one is the best for them. Even if they spend their time looking up on the internet for the reviews and recommendations, chances are they often ended up seeing the reviews and recommendations that are sponsored, or unreliable at all. There are also users also find it hard to keep up with news about technology. This is where Silicon News Garden comes in, to compile articles of electronic gadgets and news about technology only from trusted websites.

Silicon News Garden is a website that harvest and compile useful articles of electronic gadgets and news about technology from websites that are trusted, all in one place. This kind of website is known as aggregator website. The aim of this website is to help users in their decision of buying electronic gadgets, so they won’t ended up buying the electronic gadgets that are not suitable for them and to help the users to keep up with all the news about technology.
